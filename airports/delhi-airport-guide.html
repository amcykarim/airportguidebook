Airport Overview

Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL) is India's busiest airport and a major gateway to South Asia. Located in New Delhi, the airport serves millions of passengers annually and offers connections to destinations around the world.

Travel Tips

  • Arrive at least 3 hours before international departures.
  • Use the Airport Express Metro for the fastest trip into central Delhi.
  • Keep passport and travel documents easily accessible.
  • Check baggage restrictions before arriving at security.
