Airport Overview

Frankfurt Airport (FRA) is Germany's largest airport and one of Europe's busiest international aviation hubs. Located near Frankfurt city center, the airport connects travelers to destinations across Europe, Asia, North America, Africa, and the Middle East.

Travel Tips

  • Arrive at least 3 hours before international departures.
  • Use the train station inside the airport for easy travel across Germany.
  • Follow terminal signs carefully, as FRA is a large airport.
  • Check lounge access options before your flight.
