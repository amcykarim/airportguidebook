Airport Overview

John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK) is one of the busiest international airports in the United States and serves New York City. The airport handles millions of passengers every year and offers connections to destinations around the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I arrive at JFK?

Arrive at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international flights.

Does JFK have free Wi-Fi?

Yes, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the airport.
