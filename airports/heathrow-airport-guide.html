Airport Overview

Heathrow Airport (LHR) is the busiest airport in the United Kingdom and one of the world's leading international airports. Located west of Central London, Heathrow serves millions of passengers every year and connects travelers to destinations across Europe, Asia, North America, Africa and the Middle East.
