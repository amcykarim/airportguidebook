Airport Overview

Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) is one of the busiest airports in the United States and serves as a major gateway to the Pacific region. Located in Los Angeles, California, LAX connects travelers to destinations throughout North America, Asia, Europe, Oceania, and Latin America.

Travel Tips

  • Arrive at least 3 hours before international departures.
  • Expect heavy traffic around the airport during peak hours.
  • Use FlyAway buses for convenient transportation to downtown Los Angeles.
  • Check terminal information before arriving, as LAX is a large airport.
